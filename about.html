What is Foodle?
Foodle is a food guessing game inspired by other Wordle-esque daily games. The twist is - instead of guessing the word, you'll have to guess the food in 6 tries instead!

The idea was born from the developer, Rook's interest in food from other countries. Having lived in a place famous for the diversity of its cuisine, he naturally became interested in how the history and culture of other countries shaped their cuisine.

He hopes that through this game, you too can also learn more about food from all around the world!

How to play?
After every time you guess a food, hints will appear on based on three categories: "Ingredients", "Country of Origin", and "Cooking Method".

For example, if the correct dish is Pizza, and you guess Lasagna, this would appear for the "Ingredients" hint:
Cheese Tomato Pasta Minced Meat Bechamel
The Cheese and Tomato are green, because these are ingredients used in Pizza.
However, Pasta, Minced Meat, and Bechamel Sauce are red because they are used in Lasagna, but not Pizza.
This same logic is used for the "Country of Origin" and "Cooking Method" hints as well.
