How does Foodle Work?

Foodle shows you a pixelated image of a dish which you have 6 tries to guess.

Every failed guess unpixelates the image slightly, and gives you some clues.
If the clue shown is green, the ingredient is present in the dish. If the clue is red, the ingredient is not used.
The same applies for the country and cooking method clues.
